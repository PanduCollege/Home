History Department

Established in 1962, Pandu College's History Department evolved from Higher Secondary to Major Courses in 1970 and Post Graduate courses in 2021. With experienced faculty, its mission is to provide a global historical understanding, preparing students for diverse careers and fostering analytical and communication skills through workshops, seminars, and discussions. The department actively disseminates historical knowledge and information

pandu college

Political Science Department

Established in 1962, Pandu College's Political Science Department, led by Dr. K. N. Mukharjee and Prof Narayan Deka, initiated with two faculties for general classes. Evolving over the years, it now offers honors classes, introduced Human Rights Education in 2010, and gained approval for Post Graduate Classes in 2020 under Gauhati University, with a dedicated team of faculty.

Geography Department

Geography is the study of the features of Earth and the interaction of human beings with them. The geographical elements along with all resources of a nation, how influence the economy and environment, and their reciprocal relationship between them are the focal themes of the subject. The geography department was established on 1st August 1982 in Pandu College.

Hindi Department

Established in 1963, Pandu College initiated the Hindi Department with Dr. Tarakant Jha as its founding faculty, dedicated to promoting Hindi through higher studies and fostering human resources for Hindi-oriented job opportunities. Emphasizing Hindi's roles as a Link Language, Official Language, National Language, and International Language, the department currently offers both honors and general courses at the undergraduate level.

Philosophy Department

The Department of Philosophy has been one of the prominent constituents of Pandu College throughout its journey. In 1962, Logic and Philosophy was included among the subjects of study when Pandu College started its instructions. In 1963, Philosophy was introduced in the Degree pass course. Subsequently, in 1970, the Department of Philosophy started its full fledged functioning with the introduction of Honours (Major) course in Philosophy.
